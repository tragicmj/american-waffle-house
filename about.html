About American Waffle House

Our Story

Over the years

Swagata Debsharma, a MICA Alumni, has always felt a strong yearning for desserts. She was very much focused that her dessert café would be a perfect hang out zone to relax and repose. Initially, she decided to start with an ice cream parlor in 2014. But her association with Mr. Shyamal Das, an IIM Alumni reshaped the dream in a new way.

In 2016 they found Waffle in Mumbai. Swagata and Shyamal felt that waffle is going to set a new horizon in dessert world, hence decided to initiate the Entrepreneurial journey and founded ‘’American Waffle House’’. The journey of started from a 96 sq ft shop with two stool seating arrangement which now has six outlets in Kolkata.
